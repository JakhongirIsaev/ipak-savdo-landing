# Blog cover images — system + prompts

Every blog article now ships three cover images for best Google Article + social coverage:

- `public/photos/blog/<slug>-1x1.jpg` (1200x1200) — square, used in JSON-LD
- `public/photos/blog/<slug>-4x3.jpg` (1200x900) — landscape, used as the card thumbnail
- `public/photos/blog/<slug>-16x9.jpg` (1200x675) — wide, used as the article hero + Open Graph / Twitter card

`BlogPost.image` stores these as absolute `https://birliy.uz/...` URLs (required for JSON-LD/OG). The on-page `<img>` strips the origin and serves them same-origin (`lib`/`components/blog/BlogArticle.tsx`).

## Current art: programmatic editorial covers

The shipped covers are generated by `scripts/gen-blog-images.mjs` (Node + sharp). They are clean vector editorial illustrations, on BirLiy brand:

- BirLiy green + teal, soft paper background, brand check chip, no text
- Product: phone POS / receipt / QR / stacked-inventory / magnifier motifs
- AI: search-and-chat bubble + node network + spark
- Football: a tactics-board pitch + dashed arrow + a small shop "box" token + a ball

These are 100% safe: no photos, no team logos, no player likeness, no copyrighted sports imagery, no readable UI. Regenerate any time with `node scripts/gen-blog-images.mjs`.

## Alt text

Alt text for each image is the article title in the active locale (set in `BlogArticle.tsx` / `PostCard`). Titles already describe the topic in plain UZ/RU/EN, so alts are descriptive and localized automatically.
